Data Management

The Wii Options screen allows you to manage (erase) data stored in Wii system memory. The basic procedures are described below.

  • If there is not enough space available to save data used by the Wii Menu, erase outdated or unnecessary data in Wii Options.

1 Select (Wii Options).

2 Select Data Management.

3 Select a type of data.

4 Select a data icon.

5 Choose Erase.

  • indicates the size of the data (measured in blocks).

Data Management Precautions

  • Data cannot be recovered once it is erased.
  • Data cannot be moved or copied on the Wii mini console.

Important Privacy Information

Through your Wii mini console, you will be able to participate in a variety of games and activities. In order to protect your privacy and that of your family when using your Wii mini console, we strongly recommend the following:

  • You should supervise your children's use of the Wii mini console at all times and assist them with the setup of the Wii mini console.
  • Be aware that your Wii mini console may store certain personal information on your Wii Remote. By using your Wii Remote on a Wii or Wii mini console other than your own, it is possible that certain personal information about you could be shared with that other console.
  • For your protection, prior to selling or transferring your Wii console, please be sure to clean and securely remove all of your personal information, including any pictures or messages on the Wii Message Board and all other stored sensitive information in Wii system memory. Information about how to wipe your Wii mini console clean of all such information can be found under "Format Wii System Memory" (→).
  • Do not use any personal information on the Wii mini console, including the nicknames for Mii characters you create.
